Bembo Poster

Bembo typeface was create in 1495 by Francisco Griffo, an Italian punch-cutter. It was created during the Italian Renaissance period, where I mostly got my influences on for creating my Bembo poster. I took element from architectures, like the cathedral, to renaissance paintings to create my overall composition of my poster. Bembo typeface was unique to me because the serif of the typeface look ancient and cool to created unique cathedral style design.
